What is Bexie Token?

Bexie Token is the native token of the Bexie ecosystem, which aims to create a decentralized and community-driven platform centered around the popular "Bexie" meme character. The token will serve various purposes within the ecosystem, including community governance, incentivization, paying transaction fees, and access to exclusive features.

Tokenomics

The Bexie Token has been designed to be highly deflationary, with a limited supply and a burn mechanism to reduce the circulating supply over time.
